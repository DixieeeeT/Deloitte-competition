Profile-Chained Platform (PCP)

Trust company profiles become digital assets and tools on the Blockchain

Product Features

  Reliable information regulation and Security guarantee:

1. Financial Institution, SMEs, Auditing firms and other review organizations share the same chain.
2. Launching, reviewing and searching profiles can be executed at the same time
3. According to Smart Contract, all transactions followed the contract rule can be exected routinely.

  Open Investing Needs and Open Company Profile:

1. Financial institutions open their investing needs and requirements on the platform.
2. Financial institutions can set up their access and filters.
3. SMEs can open their company profile to parts or all Financial Institutions.
4. They can have more chances to exchange information and data.

  Bid Fund and e-RoadShow:

1. Open bidding platform for Financial Institution for every investing and financing projects.
2. All bidding history and transactions will be recorded and restored on the chain.
3. PCP supports VR devices and Zoom. Bidding online and Roadshow online is part of services.

  All traceable history and operations:

1. All resourced data and information will be stored at every node and at every version.
2. All transactions are open and reviewed.
3. No fraud and mistakes.
